QPG6200
#######

Key Features
************

..  youtube:: PzkRniHDVPc

**Qorvo® ’s IoT Dev Kit for QPG6200** accelerates development of products incorporating Matter (over Thread), Zigbee and Bluetooth® Low Energy wireless connectivity standards.
The solution has the best-in-class energy efficiency, right-sized security, and robust RF performance for multi-standard applications by leveraging Qorvo's ConcurrentConnect™ technology.
The kit includes certified communication stacks, turn-key reference applications, development tools and is PSA Certified Level 2 for IoT Security.

The QPG6200 is the industry’s most reliable and robust wireless multi-standard System-on-Chip. Featuring Qorvo’s **ConcurrentConnect™** technology, the QPG6200 enables multiple protocols to operate simultaneously and robust RF performance delivering improved capacity and low latency for the low-power standards. Furthermore, it leverages a secure element to keep your data and that of your customers safe.

Specifications Highlights
*************************

*  Complete solution for building multi-standard Matter™, Thread, Zigbee, and Bluetooth® Low Energy based on QPG6200
* Easily integrate ConcurrentConnect technology in your application:

   * Multi-channel enables Zigbee + Matter concurrently
   * Antenna Diversity doubles the effective range
   * Multi-radio enables BLE scanning + Matter concurrently
*  Fast time to market by leveraging certified stacks and a collection of reference apps
*  Single free-to-use build environment for Matter, Zigbee, and Bluetooth Low Energy
*  Verified interoperability with other Matter devices and infrastructure
